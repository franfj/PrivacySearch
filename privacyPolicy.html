1. What does the PrivacySearch extension do?

This extension allows users to generalize queries, and send them to a search engine automatically in local mode (without the need of an external database or infrastructure) and in real time, according to simple privacy criteria.

2. What is its goal/purpose?

Prevent web browsers from creating accurate profiles using user queries.

3. Do we collect users' information?

We do not collect any user information.
